DSAC is the Computer Science Departmental Student Advisory Committee. DSAC consists of five undergraduate CS majors who are appointed or elected by popular vote each year. For the 2018-2019 academic year, the committee consists of:

  • Jessica Ambrosio, JE '19 (she/her/hers)
  • Emmett (ECR) Chen-Ran, DC '20 (he/him/his)
  • Annie Chen, ES '19 (she/her/hers)
  • James Pastan, DC '20 (he/him/his)
  • Ke Yuxuan, BR '19 (he/him/his)

Here is the official DSAC Charter and a (soon to be updated) DSAC photo album.

Our primary task is to represent Yale CS students to the faculty and administration, meeting with the DUS and Department Chair several times a year.

Some of our other initiatives have included hosting social+food events in the Zoo, providing funding for side projects and hackathon travel, and hosting DSAC lunches with professors.

Feel free to come to us with any concerns or questions that you might have as a CS student; you can either find one of us in person or email us at dsac@cs.yale.edu!
